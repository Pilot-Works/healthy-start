5. Report a change of circumstances

You need to report changes to your circumstances so you keep receiving your Healthy Start allowance.

Your Healthy Start allowance might be stopped or reduced if you donâ€™t report a change of circumstances straight away.

Changes can include:

  • your child has been born
  • moving in with your partner
  • you or your partner start or stop receiving an eligible benefit
  • moving to a new address
  • changing your phone number or email address

Also if you are under 20 years old:

  • moving in with your parents or a guardian
  • your parent or guardian start or stop receiving an eligible benefit and you live with them
  • starting or stopping full time education

To report a change of circumstances:

  • Call the Healthy Start helpline
