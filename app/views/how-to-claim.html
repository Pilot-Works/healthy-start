4. How to claim

You apply for Healthy Start online.

Before you start

  • you can fill out this form with your health visitor or midwife
  • you can apply on behalf of your partner
  • you can apply on behalf of your daughter if they are under 18 years old and pregnant
  • the online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)
  • you can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally

You’ll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • the National Insurance number of your partner, parent/guardian, and parent/guardians partner if they live with you
  • details of the benefits your household receives
  • expected due date (if pregnant)

It takes around 10 minutes to apply.
