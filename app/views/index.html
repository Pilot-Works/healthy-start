Healthy Start is here to help you give your family the very best start in life.

If you are pregnant or have a children under the age of four and your or your family are receiving a qualifying benefit, Healthy Start can provide vouchers or a pre-paid card that can help you pay for milk, fresh or frozen fruit, fresh or frozen vegetables and infant formula milk.

To be eligible for Healthy Start you will need to be:

  • At least 10 weeks pregnant or have children under 4
  • Living in a household that receives benefits

OR

  • Under 18 years old
  • At least 10 weeks pregnant

Registering takes around 10 minutes.
You can complete this form on behalf of someone who meets the above criteria.

Start now

Before you start

  • You can fill out this form with your health visitor or midwife.
  • You can also register by post.
  • The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
  • You can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.
  • Check you are receiving one of the eligible benefits
    • Income Support
    • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
    • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance
    • Child Tax Credit (with a family income of £16,190 or less per year)
    • Universal Credit (with a family take home pay of £408 or less per month)

What you will need to apply

  • Your National Insurance number
  • The National Insurance number of your partner, parent/guardian, and parent/guardians partner if they live with you
  • Details of the benefits your household receives
  • Expected due date (If pregnant)
