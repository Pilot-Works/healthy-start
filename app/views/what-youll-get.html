2. What you'll get

If you qualify, you’ll receive a prepaid debit card in the post, which will be topped up with your Healthy Start allowance every four weeks to spend on:

  • milk
  • fresh fruit and vegetables
  • plain frozen fruit and vegetables
  • infant formula

The amount you get depends on your circumstances and the number you have parental responsibility for.

  • You get £3.10 a week if you’re pregnant
  • You will get £6.20 a week if you have a child under 1
  • You will receive £3.10 for each child you have aged between 1 and 4

If you are eligible for Healthy Start you can also get free vitamins for you when you’re pregnant and for your children.
