3. Eligibility

To be eligible for Healthy Start you will need to be:

At least 10 weeks pregnant or have children under 4 and living in a household that receives one of the benefits below

Check you are receiving one of the eligible benefits:

  • Income Support
  • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance
  • Child Tax Credit (with a family income of £16,190 or less per year)
  • Universal Credit (with a family take home pay of £408 or less per month)

You’ll also be eligible for Healthy Start if:

  • You are under 18 years old and at least 10 weeks pregnant
  • You are under 20 years old, in full time education and living in a household that receives one of the benefits listed above
